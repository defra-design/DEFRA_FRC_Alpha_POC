Report your Forest Risk Commodities due diligence

If you use forest risk commodities as part of your business, you must provide a report on your due diligence activities to be sure those commodities were legally sourced.

Businesses must file a due diligence report if they conduct commercial activities in the United Kingdom during the specified reporting period and:

  • meet the requirements for global turnover, or is a subsidiary of another business which meets those requirements
  • meet the thresholds for forest risk commodities usage

If your business will not meet the usage thresholds for the commodities you use you may be exempt from reporting.

If your business is a subsidiary of another company, you may need to provide information about their global turnover and forest risk commodity usage.

Find out who needs to report their forest risk commodities due diligence.

Businesses within the scope of the legislation can use this service to:

  • report your due diligence
  • file an exemption

Before you start

Make sure that you have:

  • reviewed the Forest Risk Commodities guidance about when to report and what information you will need to provide
  • collected the documentation to support your report or exemption
